# PRD: Post 006 — Unsupervised Discovery

**AI Engineering Lab Series | Era 1: Classic Machine Learning**

## Objective
Teach K-Means, DBSCAN, and PCA using two novel datasets where the algorithm discovers hidden structure without any labels.

## Datasets
| Dataset | Domain | Rows | Hidden Clusters |
|---|---|---|---|
| Smartwatch Activity | General (Wearables) | 4,000 | 6 activities |
| Wafer Defect Patterns | Post-Silicon Validation | 8,000 | 6 defect patterns |

## Concepts Covered
K-Means (inertia, centroids, convergence), Elbow Method, Silhouette Score, DBSCAN (eps, min_samples, core/border/noise points), PCA (explained variance, principal components, biplot), K-Means vs DBSCAN comparison, Adjusted Rand Index for cluster evaluation.
