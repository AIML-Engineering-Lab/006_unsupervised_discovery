\section{Project Overview}

I tackled discovery problems using unsupervised learning to reveal patterns in unlabeled data. I implemented K-Means, DBSCAN, and PCA on datasets related to smartwatch activity and wafer defects. The K-Means algorithm achieved significant clustering accuracy, while DBSCAN identified structure in noisy data.

\textbf{K-Means:} Cluster data by minimizing variance.

\textbf{DBSCAN:} Identify dense regions to form clusters.

\textbf{PCA:} Reduce dimensionality while preserving variance.

\textbf{Elbow Method:} Determine optimal clusters by finding the 'elbow' in the plot.

\textbf{Silhouette Score:} Evaluate cluster separation.
